(Not) Being Present

What's the point of meeting a friend or traveling if you're not present?

That's the exact same question I asked myself while traveling New Zealand. I have been to some of the most amazing places in the world with wonderful people and still would worry about what other people may think of me. I would spend so much time worrying and daydreaming about things when instead I could simply enjoy the moment, the places and the people.

We have the possibility to enjoy our lives fully by living in the present. Or instead keep worrying about what might possibly happen.

What do you choose?

Liked by everyone

Do you sometimes feel like you need to be liked by everyone? And try to hide your weaknesses and mistakes?

Well, I did. For a long time. Sometimes, I still do because I fear getting rejected.

But what's the point?!

It just keeps us from making deep connections - even with the people that accept us the way we are.

More importantly, it keeps us from accepting ourselves and living the life we always wanted to live.

So, be who you are. Accept yourself. And start living a life for yourself and not for anyone else!

Appreciation and ...

Appreciating other people is a wonderful as well as rewarding thing to do - both for the one receiving the appreciation and the one giving it.

Imagine you appreciate a friend of yours, or maybe even a stranger who is going through some troubles and anxieties.

Imagine you can be the one that lifts their mood and sparks their recovey.

Imagine the love you're going to receive for being so caring.

You have the power to help someone and make that person's day better.

So use that power of yours!

Netflix & Chill

Why is watching movies and series so popular?

(1) It gives people a break from their lives, their fears and everything that keeps them awake at night.

It feels rewarding.

But as soon as the movie ends, we solowly feel our own thoughts coming back.

(2) It gives people what they crave.

If you crave more love and romance, there are tons of movies and series out there for you.

If you crave adventure, the movie industry got your back covered as well.

So, why don't you think about your motives the next time you're about to watch a movie.

This is one important step towards finding out about yourself and becoming more self-aware.
